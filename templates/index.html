How it Works

📸

1. Photo + Location

Drop a pin on the affected area and upload a clear photo of the garbage.

🤖

2. Automated Alerts

We log the report. After 2, 4, and 7 days, our system automatically escalates the issue to the BBMP via email.

✅

3. Close with Proof

When the area is cleaned, upload an "After" photo to mark the spot as resolved. Accountability made easy.

Quick tips — Do's & Don'ts

📧 Who receives the emails?

Primary: specialswmbbmp@gmail.com

Escalation: spcomswm@bbmp.gov.in

✔️ Do's

  • Take a clear photo showing the exact location and any nearby landmark.
  • Include a brief description of the issue and any safety concerns.
  • Pin the exact spot on the map before submitting.

❌ Don'ts

  • Do not upload spam or irrelevant images.
  • Do not upload personal photos or images containing private information — these reports are emailed to officials.
  • Do not submit duplicate reports for the same spot unless conditions persist.
